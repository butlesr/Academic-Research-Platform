## Welcome! How to Join the Platform

### Step 1: Register Your Account

1. Open this link in your browser:
   **https://[your-platform-url].vercel.app/register**
   
   *(Your professor will share the actual link with you)*

2. Fill in the form:
   - **First Name / Last Name** — your actual name
   - **Email** — use your college email (preferred)
   - **Phone** — your WhatsApp number
   - **Role** — select your correct role:
     - PhD Scholar
     - PG Student (Dissertation)
     - Project Student
   - **Password** — must have uppercase + number + symbol (e.g., `Research@2024`)

3. Click **"Create Account"**

### Step 2: Verify Your Email

- Check your email inbox (also check Spam folder)
- Click the **"Verify Email Address"** button
- You'll be redirected to the login page

### Step 3: Wait for Approval

Your professor/admin will activate your account.
You'll receive an email once approved.

### Step 4: Log In

1. Go to: **https://[your-platform-url].vercel.app/login**
2. Enter your email and password
3. Click **"Sign In"**

## 📱 Using on Mobile (Android/iPhone)

**Install as App (no app store needed!):**
1. Open Chrome browser on your phone
2. Visit the platform URL
3. Tap the **three dots menu (⋮)** in Chrome
4. Tap **"Add to Home Screen"**
5. Tap **"Add"**

The app icon will appear on your home screen like a regular app!

## ✅ How to Update Your Goal Status

When your guide assigns you a goal:

1. Go to **"Goals & Tasks"** (left sidebar)
2. You'll see your goals colored by status:
   - 🔴 **Red** = Overdue (urgent!)
   - 🟡 **Yellow** = In Progress
   - 🟢 **Green** = Completed
   - 🔵 **Blue** = Submitted
   - ⚪ **Gray** = Not Started

3. Click any goal to open it
4. **Update your progress:**
   - Drag the **Progress Slider** (0% to 100%)
   - Click your status button:
     - **Start** → when you begin working
     - **Need Help** → when you're stuck
     - **Submit** → when ready for review
     - **Done ✓** → when fully complete
   - Add a **Remark** describing what you did
5. Click **"Save Changes"**

Your guide gets notified automatically! ✉️

## 💬 How to Chat with Your Guide

1. Click **"Chat"** in the sidebar
2. You'll see all your conversations
3. Click your guide's name to open direct chat
4. Type your message → Press **Enter** or click **Send**

**You can share:**
- Text messages
- Files (PDF, DOCX, images)
- Voice notes (mobile)

---

## 📤 How to Submit Assignments

1. Go to **"Assignments"** in sidebar
2. Click on the assignment
3. Read the instructions carefully
4. Click **"Submit Assignment"**
5. Upload your file (PDF, DOCX, etc.)
6. Add any comments
7. Click **"Submit"**

You'll receive your marks and feedback here.

---

## 📊 How to Track Your Attendance

1. Go to **"Attendance"** in sidebar
2. See your attendance percentage
3. **For QR attendance:** When guide shows QR code:
   - Click **"Scan QR"** button
   - Allow camera access
   - Scan the QR code shown by your professor
   - Your attendance is marked automatically!

---

## 🤖 How to Use AI Assistant

The AI can help with your research!

1. Click **"AI Assistant"** in sidebar
2. Type your question, for example:
   - *"Help me write an abstract for drug delivery research"*
   - *"What is the best methodology for my pharmaceutical study?"*
   - *"Check if this paragraph sounds academic"*
3. The AI responds instantly

**Note:** Always verify AI responses with your guide before using in thesis.

## 📜 Download Your Certificates

1. Click **"Certificates"** in sidebar
2. See all certificates issued to you
3. Click **"Download PDF"** to save
4. Verify any certificate at: `/verify/[certificate-number]`

## 🔐 Security Tips

- Never share your password with anyone
- Enable **2-Factor Authentication** (Profile → Security → Enable 2FA)
- Always log out on shared/lab computers
- Use a strong password: uppercase + number + symbol
