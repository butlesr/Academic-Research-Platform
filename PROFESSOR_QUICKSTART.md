# 👨‍🏫 Professor Quick Start Guide
## First 30 Minutes on the Platform

---

## After Login — Do These 5 Things First

---

## ✅ Task 1: Set Up Your Research Group (5 mins)

A Research Group is like a WhatsApp group for your PhD scholars.

1. Click **"Research Scholars"** in sidebar
2. Click **"Groups"** → **"Create Group"**
3. Fill in:
   - **Group Name:** "PhD Batch 2024" or "Lab A Research Group"
   - **Research Domain:** Pharmaceutics / Chemistry / etc.
   - **Description:** Brief description
4. Click **"Create"**
5. Click **"Add Members"** → Search and add your scholars by email

---

## ✅ Task 2: Register Your First Scholar's Project (5 mins)

1. Click **"Research"** → **"Projects"** → **"New Project"**
2. Fill in:
   - **Scholar:** Select from dropdown (they must have registered first)
   - **Title:** Thesis/research title
   - **Type:** PhD / M.Pharm Dissertation / Project
   - **Start Date:** Today
   - **Expected End Date:** Submission deadline
   - **Domain:** Research area
3. Click **"Create Project"**

The scholar can now see their project on their dashboard!

---

## ✅ Task 3: Assign Your First Goal (5 mins)

Think of Goals as WhatsApp tasks with deadlines.

1. Click **"Goals & Milestones"** → **"Assign Goal"**
2. Fill in:
   - **Assign To:** Select your scholar
   - **Goal Title:** "Complete Literature Review" 
   - **Priority:** High
   - **Due Date:** Set a realistic date
3. **Add Milestones** (sub-tasks):
   - Click **"AI Generate"** — AI creates 5 milestone steps automatically!
   - OR click **"+ Add"** manually:
     - "Search 50 relevant papers"
     - "Prepare reference list"
     - "Write introduction section"
     - "Get guide approval"
4. Set **Reminder Days:** 1,3,7 (sends reminders before deadline)
5. Click **"Assign Goal"**

**Scholar gets an email + app notification immediately!** 🔔

---

## ✅ Task 4: Send a Welcome Message (2 mins)

1. Click **"Chat"** in sidebar
2. Click **"+"** → **"Create Group"**
3. Name: "PhD Group 2024"
4. Add all your scholars
5. Send your first message:

```
Welcome everyone to our research platform! 🎓

This is where we will:
✅ Track your research progress
✅ Share assignments and deadlines  
✅ Communicate professionally
✅ Monitor your thesis work

Please update your profile photo and complete your profile.

Any questions — message me here directly.

- Prof. [Your Name]
```

---

## ✅ Task 5: Check Your Guide Dashboard (3 mins)

1. Click **"Dashboard"** in sidebar
2. You'll see:
   - **Scholar cards** with progress bars — see who is behind!
   - **Overdue goals** — red alerts need your attention
   - **Upcoming meetings** you've scheduled
   - **Goal status chart** — donut chart of all goal statuses
3. Click any scholar card → see their full progress timeline

---

## 📊 Weekly Routine (15 minutes every Monday)

| Action | Where |
|--------|-------|
| Check overdue goals | Dashboard → "Attention Required" |
| Review submitted work | Assignments → Filter by "Submitted" |
| Update scholar feedback | Goals → Click goal → Add guide remarks |
| Schedule weekly meeting | Meetings → "Schedule Meeting" |
| Check attendance | Attendance → Weekly report |

---

## 🎯 Goal Status Colors — What They Mean

| Color | Status | Your Action |
|-------|--------|-------------|
| 🔴 Red | Delayed / Overdue | Call/message scholar urgently |
| 🟡 Yellow | In Progress | Monitor — check next week |
| 🔵 Blue | Submitted | Review and grade |
| 🟢 Green | Completed | Mark as approved |
| 🟣 Purple | Need Help | Scholar is stuck — help them |
| ⚫ Gray | Not Started | Send reminder |

---

## 📋 Create an Assignment

1. **Assignments** → **"New Assignment"**
2. Fill: Title, Instructions, Max Marks, Due Date
3. Enable **Plagiarism Check** if needed (uses AI)
4. Enable **Rubric** to define grading criteria
5. Click **"Publish"** — scholars get notified

To grade submissions:
- **Assignments** → Click assignment → **"Submissions"**
- Click each submission → Add marks + feedback → **"Submit Grade"**

---

## 📹 Schedule Online Classes / Meetings

1. Click **"Meetings"** → **"Schedule Meeting"**
2. Fill:
   - Title, Date & Time, Duration
   - Add participants (scholars)
   - Platform: **Jitsi** (free, no account needed for participants)
3. Click **"Schedule"**
4. A meeting link is auto-generated
5. Participants get email + notification with the link

**On meeting day:**
- Click **"Join Meeting"** — opens video call directly
- No software installation needed for scholars (browser-based)

---

## 📈 Generate Reports

1. Click **"Reports"** in sidebar
2. Choose:
   - **Progress Report (PDF)** — individual scholar summary
   - **Attendance Report (Excel)** — downloadable spreadsheet
   - **Batch Performance** — comparison chart

---

## 🏆 Issue a Certificate

1. Click **"Certifications"** → **"Issue Certificate"**
2. Select:
   - Student, Certificate Type, Title
   - Optionally set expiry date
3. Click **"Issue"**
4. A professional PDF certificate is generated with:
   - QR verification code
   - Your digital signature
   - Certificate number for verification

---

## 🤖 Use AI for Research Insights

For any scholar project:
1. Open the project → Click **"AI Insights"**
2. AI analyzes progress data and gives:
   - Risk score (is this scholar at risk of delay?)
   - Predicted completion date
   - Specific recommendations
   - Personalized message for the scholar
